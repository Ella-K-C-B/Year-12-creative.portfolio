Fountain No.1


Fountain No.1 was made for a Year 11 school Big Picture Project to determine whether creating a water fountain designed to assist with promoting positive mental health was a suitable Year 12 Thesis project. It is a combination of designs from existing fountains other people had made and posted on Youtube. Below are photos of fountain No.1 including a flow chart of the build process.

Fountain No.2


Fountain No.2 was made as my Term 3 aretfact in Year 12 Big Picture as a trial for my Year 12 major fountain project. It's main purpose was to determine whether materials such as expanding foam are suitable for the fountain No.3. My other objectives were to address feedback/design suggestions I recieved from Fountain No.1. This included increasing the sound of water, adding colourful plants, making it more lightweight and using battery powers lights rather than needing a second plug.
